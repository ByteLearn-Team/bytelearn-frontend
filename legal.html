Terms of Use & Privacy Policy

Welcome to BytLearn, an AI-powered learning platform developed by VeloxCT. BytLearn has been created as part of an academic minor project with the goal of providing students an interactive, AI-driven study companion for Biology (NCERT Class 11 & 12, NEET focused).

By accessing or using BytLearn, you agree to the following Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

Terms of Use

1. Purpose

BytLearn is an educational project designed to demonstrate AI-powered study support. While it aims to provide helpful study tools, it is not a replacement for teachers, textbooks, or coaching institutes.

2. Account Registration

  • Users register with name, email, and password.
  • An OTP verification system ensures that the email belongs to the student.
  • After verification, users log in using their email and password.

3. Features Offered

  • Summaries & Flashcards – Pre-prepared content stored in the database.
  • Quizzes – AI-powered, time calculated with performance tracking.
  • Doubt Solving – Real-time AI answers to subject-related questions.
  • Profile Analytics – Insights on performance, including quiz scores, time taken, weak/strong areas, and common doubt topics.

4. Acceptable Use

You agree to:

  • Use BytLearn only for personal learning.
  • Provide accurate details during registration.
  • Not misuse the AI responses for cheating in exams.
  • Not attempt to hack, disrupt, or damage the platform.

5. Educational Disclaimer

  • Quizzes and doubt answers are AI-generated.
  • We cannot guarantee 100% accuracy. Students should always cross-check with NCERT textbooks.
  • Exam outcomes and learning results depend on multiple factors; BytLearn is only a support tool.

7. Changes to Service

Features may be updated, improved, or modified as the project develops.
